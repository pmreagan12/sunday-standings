Ranking Methodology & Differential Engine

1. USGA Handicap Differential Formula

Every time you post a round, Sunday Standings computes your official USGA Handicap Differential using the standard formula:

Differential = (Gross Score − Course Rating) × (113 / Slope Rating)

This normalized score allows golfers playing difficult championship layouts to be accurately ranked alongside golfers playing shorter resort courses.

2. Pro-Am Interleaved Global Rankings

How do you compare to Scottie Scheffler or Rory McIlroy? OWGR professionals earn points based on field strength and tournament finishes. Sunday Standings maps amateur USGA differentials to an equivalent ranking scale:

  • Scratch or Better (+2.0 to 0.0 diff): Equivalent to Korn Ferry Tour / PGA Tour qualifying threshold (Rank 1 - 250).
  • Single Digit Handicap (1.0 to 9.0 diff): Highly competitive amateur tier, interleaved with Tour veterans and European Challenge tour players.
  • Mid-Handicap (10.0 to 20.0 diff): Active club players and weekend syndicate competitors.
